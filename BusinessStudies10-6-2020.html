50 word summary

In operations management, there are costs and scale of production.There are fixed costs, which is costs that do not change with the number of items sold/produced.Fixed costs include the following: -Rent of factory, -Insurance, -Bank fees, -Management salaries, and -staff cost. We also have variable costs, which is costs that are directly proportional with the number of products or items sold/produced/manufactured. Examples are: -Raw Materials,-Electricity and gas,and -Shipping costs.
